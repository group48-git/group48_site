Integrity Assessment Service







The Integrity Assessment is a procedure which is designed to identify employees and candidates who are prone to dishonest and counterproductive behaviors, leading to organizational delinquency. The assessment outcome enables decision makers in the organization to obtain a comprehensive profile of the employee or candidate, and information about different integrity issues arising from the individual's attitudes, values and behaviors.


ERA‘s Integrity Assessment was designed by security experts and psychologists with vast and prolonged experience within this field. It has been successfully implemented throughout the years in leading Israeli security organizations as well as large-scale organizations abroad.



The Process


Era’s Integrity Assessment process is designed in a modular way, so it can be applied independently from other assessment procedures and still provide the organization with a focused report regarding an individual's integrity and credibility level. The assessment is carried out individually, although can include a group simulation and provide a more profound report with regards to the individual's interpersonal characteristics.

The Method


A modular procedure that includes some or all of the following:


Computerized credibility questionnaire

Personality and behavioral questionnaires

Situational reliability tests

In depth occupational interview

A group situational test (optional)

Who is it for?


Organizations that require a high level of credibility and integrity (Financial sector, insurance companies, Casinos, etc)

Defense industries

Suppliers and contractors entering the organization

Employees facing promotion / tenure status, and more

Employees dealing with big sums of money and sensitive organizational information

The Product


A profile report that specifically addresses the relevant clusters of assessment in the realms of personality tendencies, personal values and attitudes, interpersonal characteristics, and credibility score.

Our Clients


Government offices, public sector organizations, security agencies, private sector businesses
