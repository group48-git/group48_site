City Safety Solution







The Need for Turnkey Safe City Solutions


Driven by numerous political, economical and ecological global factors, the security market is facing an increasing demand on safe city solutions. However, few solutions specialize in the efficient and flexible protection and management of urban areas.


System integrators have yet to choose from isolated systems to put together a complete solution that includes:


  • Cameras

  • Video Management Software (VMS)

  • Physical Infrastructure Information Management (PSIM) software

  • A mobile solution

  • And many others required by their actual projects


System integrators need independent ecosystems that allow for the configuration of fully integrated turnkey solutions



CRISP: Independent, Modular, Comprehensive


CRISP Safe City provides system integrators all they need.


System integrators need to concentrate on their core business: project management, constructions, installations, and services.


CRISP SC is an independent ecosystem offering all to integrators - from software to cameras to mobile and network devices - allowing them to concentrate on their core business.


CRISP SC includes the full range of command and control:


  • An open and camera-independent VMS that is able to seamlessly integrate with any sensor

  • A dynamic and intelligent GIS map with full-fledged incident management features:

    • The reporting engine allows sophisticated scheduled and ad-hoc business reports

    • Due to its outstanding flexibility, it can be integrated into any kind of IT landscape


CRISP SC is a total state-of-the-art ecosystem:


  • With the PSIM at its heart, it comes with the best-fitting cameras, sensors, security systems, and support services


CRISP SC is in the vanguard of supporting security operators in order to detect, prevent, and react to security threats:


  • Allows for smooth urban operations in day-to-day business

  • Enables handling of planned events, as well as unplanned incidents, emergencies, and crises



The Difference is in the Software


CRISP SC has a uniquely simple graphical user interface (GUI). Driven by the motto “security made easy”, it is arguably the most intuitive system in the world, re-defining simplicity by allowing plug and play, practically without any training at all.


Based on end-user stories:


  • In the GUI, everything is accessible within mouse clicks, drag and drop, or the turning of a joystick, fully supporting user interactions in typical urban management scenarios


With dynamic tracking and routing of mobile security patrols:


  • CRISP allows dynamic tracking and routing of mobile units such as security patrols or emergency vehicles, as well as sophisticated communications between the control centres and the field units


Support for any sensor:


  • The software integrates with any sensors in the field: cameras of various specifications, mobile devices, fire detection, alarm systems and access control systems, and many more


A powerful plug-in for all kinds of analytics:


  • CRISP PSIM includes a powerful plug-in for all kinds of analytics. This allows us to choose the best fit analytics module for each user scenario




State of Your City - Everything at Your Fingertips at Any Time


Trends, statistics, benchmarks, information:


  • CRISP SC offers a wide range of reporting facilities

  • Users can pre-define the events and scenario that they wish to receive regular statistics about: traffic accidents, intrusions, assaults, or anything else

  • CRISP allows a variety of reports to be made (including ad-hoc), based on various schedules


To be sent to the decision makers:


  • CRISP SC acts as the eyes and ears of the city leaders

  • Using various charts and geographical reports (e.g. reduction of traffic incidents in an area over the last year, growth of shop robberies over the last quarter), it allows easy benchmarking of decisions with respect to time and place

  • Decision makers have their key figures available, at any time, right at their fingertips
