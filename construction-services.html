Company Overview


Bcont is a startup Israeli software company, providing a web-based finance control solution for construction companies. It has developed a system that provides an effective and transparent response to the entire chain of management and stakeholders in projects.


The team includes experienced finance and technology individuals, who have implemented the system in over 30 projects.

To be the market leader in the financial management of construction projects, make a real-time connection between construction companies and their construction sites, and to become the leading network systems provider in the construction industry.

The Problem


Execution of a construction project covers a wide range of factors, starting with a large number of contractors through the management of many milestones, up to payment management.


Project managers must have full control of the budget, as the information does not come in real-time from construction sites.


When the payment is due, disagreements often arise between various contractors about when it should be due.


Lack of control over the project creates irregularities in the budget, which sometimes leads to double payments or payments for milestones that were not actually implemented.


Project managers often see actual expenditures reported long after their order or project has been put through.

The Solution


Applies to the entire process of managing a construction project, from the signing of a contract to that project’s completion.


The solution is a SaaS system that provides effective and transparent responses to the entire chain of management and stakeholders in these projects.


This system covers:


Project manager guidelines


Project milestones


Real-time accounting management


Raw material control


Cash flow control


Budget control
