This solution is the world’s first self-learning solar water heater that learns the user's showering habits as a means to improve power utilization and free the client from ever dealing with technical or operational issues.

Once purchased, the device is then delivered to the customer's house and automatically calls for a nearby technician to perform installation. The customer doesn’t have to touch a thing!

If it ever encounters a malfunction, it will automatically notify a technician and call for repair.

This device’s autonomous characteristics were designed for the sole purpose of minimizing operational expenses such as customer support, in-house technicians and operations management, all of which are almost entirely unnecessary.

Technicians will be employed as freelancers and will be paid on a per visit basis.

Since the device automatically detects faults and calls for a technician, the number of customer and technical support personnel will be kept to a minimum.
